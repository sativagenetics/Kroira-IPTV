KROIRA IPTV Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

KROIRA IPTV is a Windows desktop IPTV player. It does not provide channels, playlists, content, or user credentials. Users are expected to add and manage their own content sources.

What the app does

The app can access user-provided IPTV sources, stream URLs, and XMLTV/EPG data in order to display and play content inside the application.

Data storage

KROIRA IPTV may store app-related data locally on the user's device, including source configuration, favorites, playback progress, and continue-watching information.

Personal data

KROIRA IPTV does not sell personal data to advertisers. KROIRA IPTV does not act as a content provider.

Network access

The app uses internet access only to connect to user-provided media sources, poster artwork, and EPG/XMLTV endpoints.
